# Finance Business Rules

Customer Revenue means the sum of all invoice total amounts excluding cancelled invoices.

Invoice Amount is defined as the monetary value charged to a customer before payment.

Customer Number must always be unique.

Cancelled invoices must not be included in revenue calculations.

The Revenue KPI measures total recognised customer revenue by month.

The finance process uses finance_invoices_sample.csv.invoice_id and finance_invoices_sample.csv.total_amount.
